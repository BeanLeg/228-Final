Hi, I'm Blake. I'm currently going for my Bachelor of Global Business and Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo with a minor in Economics.

I have a huge passion for creative storytelling and I believe every design should tell a story, no matter how big or small the project.

I draw a lot of inspiration for my work from TV shows and anime. Some examples are Breaking Bad, Andor, and Attack on Titan. These shows in particular showed me how much complexity and deep meaning there can be behind beautiful works of art.

In addition to my love for design, I have a strong background in business and technology. Business wise, I have experience in economics, accounting and marketing. And on the tech side I have experience in basic programming and creating websites.
