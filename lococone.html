Chick'nCone - A Business Re-Brand

November 2025

This re-brand was a project for my GBDA 202 Design course. We were tasked with completely redesigning the brand of a business. I landed on Chick'nCone - a unique chicken shop franchise. I combined this assignment with my GBDA 228 Web Design class, redoing their website as well.

Fun fact: The Waterloo location of Chick'nCone closed down 2 weeks before I finished this project.
